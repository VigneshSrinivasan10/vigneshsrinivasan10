Vignesh Srinivasan

I am an Senior Applied Scientist at Zalando Research in Berlin, Germany. My research interests include score-based generative models and robustness of deep neural networks. My current focus at Zalando is to work on innovative machine learning solutions to bypass the expensive process of fashion studio photography. I prototype the generation of high resolution digital humans wearing curated outfits using generative models.

At Zalando, I work with Nikolay Jetchev and Tofigh Naghibi. Previously, I worked as a researcher at the Fraunhofer Heinrich Hertz Institute and Technische Universität Berlin with Wojciech Samek and Shinichi Nakajima. I received my PhD in machine learning under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Klaus-Robert Müller.
